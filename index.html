FROM KINESIOTAPE TO POSTURE CLOTHING

Posture corrective clothing activates the back muscles, shoulders and shoulder blades without bracing your muscles or joints. The technology behind ActivePosture's posture clothing is inspired by Kinesiotape - a treatment method often used by physiotherapists and chiropractors to treat pain and tension in the neck, shoulders and back. Kinesiotaping is also frequently used by professional athletes to stimulate specific muscle groups and for posture correction.
